# Architecture Notes (product/UX vision)

**Product/UX vision** — how the app should feel and what value it delivers. (Technical decomposition lives in `docs/ARCHITECTURE_NOTES.md`.)

---

The App should feel clean, clear and crisp and set up as if it can "read your mind" when it comes to your next action and how to sort your latest additions in terms of ideas, to-do's, outcomes, opportunities, and daily habits.  It should be easy to enter new tasks into and categorize, prioritize and schedule them.  The App should intuitively provide the feeling that "If I put my tasks, ideas, and brainstorms into this app, it will organize them, compare them against my desired outcomes in life, and all of the other tasks, items and projects I have and then feed me the next, best bite-size action I can take that will move me toward my outcomes the most."  The value of this app is to allow for brain-dumps, clearing my personal meat-based RAM, and saving me the time of figuring out what I need to do next, so I can use that time to actually do the next thing that needs to be dome the most.
